Buying a Domain

Feb 9th, 2012

I recently bought www.parasnarang.com from NameCheap.
NameCheap provides a reliable domain hosting service among others.

GoDaddy initially showed support to SOPA, so many users boycotted GoDaddy and transferred their domains to other providers. To transfer your domain from GoDaddy to NameCheap, read here.

I have also bought domains from BigRock and HIOX India in the past. I haven’t been thoroughly satisfied with HIOX India, but BigRock is good and reliable.

Before buying a domain or transferring a domain to NameCheap, be sure to look for coupons. NameCheap regularly offers discounts in the form of coupons.
Check www.namecheapcoupons.com regularly to find out the details of the most recent coupons and offers.

If you decide to signup to NameCheap, use my referral link so that I get some free credits from them.

Lots of Blog Posts Coming Up!

Jan 10th, 2012

Now I am in the sixth semester of BTech Computer Science Engineering at VIT University.
This semester I have taken a course named “Internet and Web Programming”. Since I have some decent experience in Web programming already, I am looking forward to this course.

It is under FEP. FEP is Faculty Empowerment Program. Under this scheme, the faculty is free to design his own Examination and Grading structure. This means less exams in which I have to mug up and vomit! :)

So, For Internet and Web Programming course, I have to do a mini project, make a video tutorial and maintain a blog among other things. Since I already have a blog, I’l continue with this one. I am supposed to update 5 blog posts per month. 

Hopefully this will help me overcome my laziness and make me a regular blogger!

Life 101

Dec 23rd, 2010


Thinking. 
A random person once said that thinking is bad for health. 
One’s instant reaction is ‘what rubbish!!’. 
One has to think & make plans for the future. One also has to think about the past and rectify the mistakes that were made. How can life progress without thinking??


Then again, excess of everything is harmful. Excess of thinking causes Stress. And most of the times things don’t work out as planned. Change is inevitable.


Hence, My funda of life is simple.
Dream big. Think less. Do more. Enjoy Life! :-)
